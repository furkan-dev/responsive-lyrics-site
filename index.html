Unsainted Hakkında

“Unsainted” is the first single being released from Slipknot’s album We Are Not Your Kind. The track talks about depression, as vocalist Corey Taylor has suffered with it for many years, but he’s “finally holding on to letting go. ”

When talking about the album in 2018, Corey Taylor said what completely represents this track: It’s complicated, it’s dark, it’s heavy, it’s heavy, it’s melodic, it’s fierce, it’s angry and it’s raw as hell. It’s going to be talking about a lot of things that people are going to need in their life.
